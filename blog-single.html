About Livsugar

Livsugar

LIVSUGAR, made from Stevia, is a 100% natural, chemical-free sweetener that replaces the sugar intake from your daily diet. This revolutionary product highly aids in preventing diabetes, tooth decay and weight loss. It can be utilized in food and beverages, confectioneries, pharmaceuticals and in any food industry where sweeteners are utilized. This 100% natural sweetener is superior to its artificial counterparts by virtue of being purely herbal and not a chemical derivative. It has zero calories, zero carbohydrates and again, a glycemic index of zero.

LIVSUGAR is a sugar substitute made from three main ingredients, stevia leaf extract, erythritol and other natural flavors. To make LIVSUGAR Natural Sweetener, we first take the sweetest part of the stevia leaf and extract its natural flavor. But stevia leaf extract is 200 times sweeter than sugar, so to balance the sweetness we add a natural sugar alcohol called erythritol. Then we add a special recipe of natural flavors to create great-tasting, zero calorie sweetness.
